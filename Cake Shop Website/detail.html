Product Description

Our Chocolate Delight Cake is a masterpiece of confectionery art, crafted with passion and precision by our expert pastry chefs. Each layer is baked to perfection using premium Belgian dark chocolate, creating a rich and indulgent flavor profile that chocolate lovers dream about.

The cake features multiple layers of moist chocolate sponge, generously filled with silky chocolate ganache and finished with a glossy chocolate coating. Every cake is decorated with elegant chocolate shavings, fresh berries, and edible gold accents, making it not just delicious but also a stunning centerpiece for your celebration.

We use only the finest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. Our cakes contain no artificial preservatives or additives, ensuring you get the purest, most authentic chocolate experience. Each cake is baked fresh to order and can be customized with your choice of message, decorations, and dietary requirements.

Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, or simply treating yourself to something special. Order 24 hours in advance for same-day pickup or delivery within our service area.

Ingredients

Cake Base:

Premium Belgian dark chocolate (72% cocoa), all-purpose flour, organic cane sugar, farm-fresh eggs, pure butter, whole milk, vanilla extract, baking powder, baking soda, salt

Chocolate Ganache Filling:

Belgian dark chocolate, heavy cream, butter, vanilla extract, pinch of sea salt

Frosting & Decoration:

Dark chocolate coating, chocolate shavings, fresh seasonal berries, edible gold leaf, fondant decorations (optional)

Allergen Information:

Contains: Eggs, Dairy (milk, butter, cream), Gluten (wheat flour), Tree nuts (may contain traces). Manufactured in a facility that also processes peanuts and soy products.
